CoWork.io

SaaS platform to manage coworking spaces

CoWork.io is a while label tool that allows you to create an e-commerce platform fully integrated in your own website

Coworkers can access real-time availabilities of coworking spaces

They get a 100% online experience to book their space, purchase additionnal services or request information

CoWork.io includes social features to give coworkers the opportunity to get to know each other, connect and even work together
